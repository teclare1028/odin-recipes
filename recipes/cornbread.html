Cornbread

Description

Cornbread is a real treat with chili, ribs, or barbecued chicken. You could even use this sweet, homemade cornbread to make stuffing for your Thanksgiving feast!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cornmeal
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Whisk the dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Add the wet ingredients and whisk.
  3. Pour in a prepared cake pan.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
