Mashed Potatoes

Description

Made with Idaho potatoes, milk, butter, and garlic, this go-to recipe for simple, homemade mashed potatoes is a classic holiday dish.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk>
  • Butter
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes.
  2. Heat the milk and butter until melted.
  3. Mash the potatoes.
  4. Blend with a mixer until smooth and creamy.
  5. Season to taste.
